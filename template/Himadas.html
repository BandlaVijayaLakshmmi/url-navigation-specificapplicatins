Himadas

Hima Das (born 9 January 2000), nicknamed Dhing Express,[7] is an
Indian sprinter from the state of Assam. She holds the current Indian national record in 400 meters
with a timing of 50.79 s that she clocked at the 2018 Asian Games in Jakarta, Indonesia.
She is the first Indian athlete to win a gold medal in a track event at the IAAF World U20 Championships.
She was appointed as a Deputy Superintendent of Police (DSP) in Assam Police[8] under the state's Integrated Sport Policy.[citation needed]
